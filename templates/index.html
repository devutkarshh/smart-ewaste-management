⚙️ How It Works

1
Report: User submits e-waste item details — category, brand, condition, age, department. System auto-classifies it.
2
QR Tag: A unique QR code is generated and assigned to the item. User downloads and attaches it to the device.
3
Admin Approval: Admin reviews the item, approves it, and assigns a certified recycling vendor with a pickup date.
4
Vendor Pickup: Vendor scans the QR code (or enters item ID), marking it "Collected". Status updates instantly.
5
Final Disposal: Item is disposed/recycled. Entire lifecycle recorded for compliance and analytics dashboards.
